The Best Cake Recipe

This is my favorite chocolate cake recipe. It is delicious and requires no eggs or milk and can be made in just one bowl!

Source: King Arthur's Flour

Ingredients

  • 9" Round Pan, 2" Deep
  • 1 1/2 Cups Flour
  • 1 Cup Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup Unsweetened Cocoa
  • 1/2 Tsp Salt
  • 1 Tsp Baking Soda
  • 1 Tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Tbsp Cider Vinegar
  • 1/3 Cup Vegetable Oil
  • 1 Cup Water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and lightly grease pan
  2. Whisk dry ingredients in bowl
  3. Add wet ingredients, whisk until it has a batter texture
  4. Pour into pan and bake for 30-35 minutes

Here is the final product! Tastes great with cherry jam and whipped cream for a cheaper alternative to black forest cake.
